Honasa Consumer Ltd

Honasa Consumer Ltd invests ₹10 crore in Fang Oral Care

•

Honasa Consumer Ltd invested ₹10 crore in Fang Oral Care, a Mumbai-based startup specializing in teeth whitening and oral wellness products.

•

The funding will support research and development, expansion of the product portfolio, and scaling digital presence across e-commerce platforms and quick commerce channels.

•

Varun Alagh, Chairman and CEO of Honasa Consumer, said the founders’ category expertise and vision could "transform the oral care market" through scientifically backed aspirational products.

•

Founded in 2022 by Ankit Agarwal, Ashutosh Jaiswal, and Jitendra Arora, Fang distributes its products via its website, Amazon, Flipkart, and quick commerce channels.

Swiggy

Swiggy relocates Bengaluru headquarters to Whitefield

•

Swiggy will relocate its Bengaluru headquarters from Embassy Tech Village on ORR to Sumadhura Capitol Towers in Whitefield as its six-year lease at the current site comes up for renewal.

•

The move addresses growing traffic congestion and frequent monsoon waterlogging along the ORR corridor, aligning with a broader Bengaluru startup trend to seek less congested office locations.

•

Improved metro connectivity and lower real estate costs have made Whitefield a preferred office hub, prompting companies like TCS and Airbus to expand in the area.

•

Bengaluru recorded the highest office leasing activity among India’s top nine cities with 5.1 million sq. ft. leased in April–June 2025, driven largely by small-sized deals.
